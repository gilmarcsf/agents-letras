Choose one dominant operating mode.

- **High-trust**
  Use for regulated, safety-critical, privacy-sensitive, or money-sensitive products.
- **Operational**
  Use for task-heavy software where speed, orientation, and decision support matter most.
- **Expressive**
  Use for identity-led products where differentiation and mood are part of the value.
- **Editorial**
  Use for narrative surfaces that unfold information or atmosphere in sequence.
- **Conversion**
  Use for pages where the main job is signup, booking, purchase, waitlist, lead capture, or contact sales.
- **Creation**
  Use for editors, builders, design tools, content tools, and AI generation surfaces where users manipulate an artifact.

If two modes seem valid, pick the one that governs the user's main job, then borrow carefully from the second.
